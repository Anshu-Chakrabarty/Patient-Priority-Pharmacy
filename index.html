Our Story

Elevating Patient-Centered Care

We founded Patient Priority Pharmacy on a simple belief: every patient deserves a pharmacist who truly knows them.

🏥
Our Mission

More Than a Pharmacy. A Healthcare Partner.

Founded in 2009, Patient Priority Pharmacy was born from a frustration with the transactional, impersonal nature of big-box pharmacy chains. Our founders — both licensed pharmacists — believed that pharmacy care could and should be transformative.

Today we serve over 15,000 patients across South Florida, offering everything from routine prescription fills to highly complex specialty therapies — all with the personal touch that makes us different.

We believe that a great pharmacist isn't just someone who counts pills — they're a clinician, an advocate, and a partner in your health journey.

Simple & Easy

How Patient Priority Works

Getting the best pharmacy care shouldn't be complicated. We've designed every step to be seamless and stress-free.

1

Send Us Your Prescription

Getting your prescription to us is easy — choose whichever method is most convenient for you:

  • Ask your doctor to send an e-prescription directly to Patient Priority Pharmacy
  • Drop off your paper prescription in person at our store
  • Call us and we'll coordinate a transfer from your current pharmacy
  • Use our online transfer form and we'll handle the rest
2

Insurance Verification & Savings Check

Before we fill anything, we proactively work to minimize your cost:

  • Verify your insurance benefits and coverage in real time
  • Check for manufacturer coupons and patient assistance programs
  • Compare brand vs. generic pricing
  • Apply any available discount programs (GoodRx, etc.)
3

Clinical Review by a Licensed Pharmacist

Every prescription undergoes a rigorous clinical review before dispensing:

  • Drug interaction screening against your complete medication profile
  • Allergy and contraindication checks
  • Dosage verification with prescribing guidelines
  • Consultation with your prescriber if any concerns are flagged
4

Dispensing & Pharmacist Counseling

Your medication is carefully prepared and you receive thorough counseling:

  • Verification by a second pharmacist (double-check system)
  • Patient-friendly medication information sheet provided
  • Pharmacist counseling on proper use, side effects, and storage
  • Answers to all your questions — no rush, no limit
5

Pick Up or Get It Delivered

Choose how you want to receive your medication:

  • Pick up in-store — typically ready in 30 minutes or less
  • Free same-day delivery within our service area
  • Mail delivery for ongoing maintenance medications
  • Curbside pickup available for mobility-limited patients
6

Ongoing Support & Refill Management

Our relationship doesn't end at the counter — we stay engaged:

  • Proactive refill reminders via phone, text, or email
  • Medication adherence check-ins for chronic conditions
  • Annual medication therapy management reviews
  • 24/7 pharmacist access for urgent questions
